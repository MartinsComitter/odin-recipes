Description

Lasagna is a italian dish consists of lasagnaleafs, bolognese prepared in the oven.

  • miced meat
  • mashed tomatoes
  • lasagnaleafs
  • spices
  1. cook meat in mashed tomatoes
  2. cook lasagnaleafs
  3. add spices
  4. put the cooked meat between the lasagnaleafs with multiple layers in a casserole dish
  5. spread some cheese on the top layer of lasagnaleafs
  6. put everything in the oven for half an hour
